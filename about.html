Builder. Tinkerer. Developer.

I turn curiosity into code and problems into solutions.

It started 4 years ago with a simple question: "How does this server actually work?"

I began by messing around with Minecraft and GTA server configs, breaking things, fixing them, and realizing I wanted to build my own tools from scratch. That curiosity quickly evolved from simple scripts into full-scale backend systems.

Now, I focus on building things that people actually use. Whether it's managing high-performance game nodes at Shonnodes or automating communities with Artic, I love the challenge of making complex systems run smoothly.

I'm not just writing code; I'm building reliable platforms and useful tools for real communities.
